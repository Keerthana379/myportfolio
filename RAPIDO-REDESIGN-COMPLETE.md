## Key Design Decisions

### 1. Color Psychology
- **Yellow**: Energy, speed, optimism (perfect for ride-hailing)
- **Black**: Premium, professional, trustworthy
- **White**: Clean, simple, modern

### 2. Brand Consistency
- Matches actual Rapido app screenshots
- Uses their signature yellow prominently
- Black/yellow contrast for readability
- Clean, minimal design language

### 3. Visual Hierarchy
- Yellow draws attention to important actions
- Black provides structure and contrast
- White space for breathing room
- Clear information architecture

### 4. User Experience
- High contrast for readability
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Consistent color coding
- Professional appearance

## Design System

### Primary Actions
- Background: #FFD500 (Yellow)

- Shadow: rgba(255, 213, 0, 0.3)

### Secondary Actions
- Background: #fff (White)
- Border: #e0e0e0 (Gray)
- Text: #000 (Black)

### Status Indicators
- Active/Live: #FFD500 (Yellow)
- Waiting: #FFD500 (Yellow)
- Complete: #FFD500 (Yellow)

### Maps
- Background: #f0f0f0 (Light gray)
- Pins: Black with yellow centers
- Routes: Dashed gray lines
